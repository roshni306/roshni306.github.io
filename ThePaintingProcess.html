The Painting Process

Pre-painting consultation

After you fill in an inquiry, I will get in touch with you for a first consultation! We will discuss the date’s availability, and I hope to listen about your special day and what you’re looking for. I will ask you guided questions and show you examples to gain inspiration from to gain a concrete understanding of your vision. I’ll walk you through exactly how your vision will be brought to life and how any concerns will be taken care of. Since we are (literally!) working with a blank canvas the customisations are endless! So, I am always happy to extend a consult or have a second in-depth round to explore more ideas – art styles, lighting options, any elements to emphasise or minimise – with you to your hearts content. We will keep in touch till the wedding date to include any additional ideas that may arise or to accommodate event changes. 

Wedding day

My setup and the space I need is minimal. I need an approximately 1.5m^2 area to set up my portable easel, lamp, and other equipment. The area needs to face the scene being painted, though after approximately 2 hours I am able to resume painting in any location. I also need one running water source (e.g. bathroom or kitchen sink) to discard and replenish paint water.

Apart from this, tools and equipments will be entirely provided myself and taken care of. I ensure that my setup is highly presentable and is visually harmonious with your wedding decor.
